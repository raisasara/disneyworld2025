ğŸ”„ What is Rider Swap?

Rider Swap is FREE and a game-changer for families!

How it works:
1. Whole family goes to ride entrance together
2. Tell Cast Member you want Rider Swap
3. Group A (e.g., Dad + 10-year-old) rides while Group B (Mom + little ones) waits
4. When Group A finishes, Group B (Mom + 10-year-old) rides with NO WAIT!
5. Your 10-year-old gets to ride TWICE! ğŸ‰

Works with Lightning Lane! If you have LL, Group A uses it, Group B still skips the line!
